About Us

The brand name of dairy products Yogi was born in 1995, at Nagoda, a very remote village in Kalutara district. From the humble beginning as a small-scale domestic enterprise, Yogi has become one of the most respected and trusted brands of dairy products in Sri Lanka today.

VISION

To A world where Yoghurt is a daily essential, nourishing bodies and souls.

MISSION

To create a healthier, happier world by providing premium, natural yoghurt products that delight taste buds and boost well-being.
